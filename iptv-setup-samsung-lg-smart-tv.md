## Which App to Use

Samsung and LG smart TVs don't support sideloading the way Android devices do, so your app choice depends on your TV's operating system:

| TV Platform | Recommended App | Available From |
|---|---|---|
| Samsung Tizen | Smart IPTV, IPTV Smarters | Samsung App Store / sideload |
| LG webOS | Smart IPTV, SS IPTV | LG Content Store / sideload |
| Android TV / Google TV | TiviMate, IPTV Smarters Pro | Google Play Store |

## Setup on Samsung Smart TV

### Option A: Smart IPTV App

1. On your Samsung TV, open the **Samsung App Store**
2. Search for **Smart IPTV** and install it
3. Open the app — note the **MAC address** displayed on screen
4. On your phone or computer, visit the Smart IPTV activation website
5. Enter your TV's MAC address and your M3U playlist URL
6. Click **Send** — the playlist is pushed to your TV
7. Restart the Smart IPTV app on your TV — your channels appear

### Option B: IPTV Smarters (via USB Sideload)

If Smart IPTV is not available in your region:

1. On a computer, download the IPTV Smarters APK for Tizen
2. Copy the APK to a USB drive
3. Insert the USB drive into your Samsung TV
4. Open the **My Files** app on the TV and navigate to the USB drive
5. Select the APK and install it
6. Open IPTV Smarters and choose **Login with Xtream Codes API**
7. Enter your server URL, username, and password

## Setup on LG Smart TV

### Option A: SS IPTV (Free from LG Content Store)

1. On your LG TV, open the **LG Content Store**
2. Search for **SS IPTV** and install it
3. Open the app and go to **Settings** → **Content** → **External Playlists**
4. Select **Add** and enter your M3U playlist URL
5. Save and return to the main screen — your channels load automatically

### Option B: Smart IPTV

Follow the same MAC-address method described above for Samsung — it works identically on LG webOS.

## Setup on Android TV / Google TV

Android TV models (Sony, TCL, Hisense, Xiaomi, Chromecast with Google TV) have full Google Play Store access:

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your TV
2. Search for **TiviMate** and install it
3. Open TiviMate → **Add Playlist** → **Xtream Codes**
4. Enter your server URL, username, and password
5. Your channel list loads within seconds

## Tips for Smart TV IPTV Setup

- **Wired connection is always better.** Smart TVs have weaker Wi-Fi antennas than phones or laptops. Use an ethernet cable for 4K streaming.
- **Check your TV's DNS settings.** If channels load slowly, try switching to Google DNS (8.8.8.8) or Cloudflare DNS (1.1.1.1) in your TV's network settings.
- **Update your TV firmware.** Manufacturers frequently patch streaming and network bugs.
- **Restart the app if the EPG is empty.** EPG data loads on first boot and can take 30–60 seconds on large channel lists.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| "No channels found" | Verify your M3U URL or Xtream credentials are entered correctly. Check for typos in the server address. |
| App not in the store | Your region may not carry it. Sideload via USB or use an alternative app from the list above. |
| Buffering on 4K channels | Switch to a wired connection. Lower the stream quality in app settings if your connection is under 25 Mbps. |
| Remote control lag in the app | Close other apps running in the background. Restart the TV to free up memory. |
